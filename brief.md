## FAQs

### FAQ 1
- **Question:** what is AEO and why should my Web3 project care?
- **Answer:** Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is structuring your content so AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude cite your protocol when users ask about your vertical. 73% of B2B buyers now use AI assistants for research. Less than 1% of Web3 companies are optimized for this. Early movers compound their advantage.

### FAQ 2
- **Question:** how is this different from traditional SEO?
- **Answer:** Traditional SEO gets you ranked on Google page 1. AEO gets you cited in ChatGPT's answer. SEO is 25 years old. AEO is 2 years old. Guess which one has less competition. We do both: programmatic SEO for Google traffic plus AEO for AI citations.

### FAQ 3
- **Question:** what does the 60-day guarantee mean?
- **Answer:** If you don't see measurable results (AI citations, organic traffic growth, site performance improvement) within 60 days, you get $5,000 back. No questions, no hoops.

### FAQ 4
- **Question:** do you work with all Web3 verticals?
- **Answer:** Yes. DeFi, RWA/tokenization, L1/L2 chains, DAOs, GameFi, DePIN, and NFT-Fi. Each vertical gets specific keyword targeting, competitive analysis, and content strategy based on their market dynamics.

### FAQ 5
- **Question:** what's the pricing?
- **Answer:** We offer three tiers: AI Audit (2-3 weeks, one-time), AI Sprint (45-60 days, monthly retainer), and AI Transformation (3-6 months, full-stack deployment). No pricing on the website because every Web3 project is different. Book a call and we'll scope it for your specific situation.

### FAQ 6
- **Question:** who does the technical delivery?
- **Answer:** Every AI system I deploy is production-grade, built by a team of AI engineers with 50+ deployments across 16 industries. You get Web3-native strategy from me backed by enterprise AI engineering.
